Our Story

About Lumière

Lighting up homes with handcrafted luxury since 2018. We believe every moment deserves a beautiful scent.

From a Small Workshop to Your Home

Lumière was founded in 2018 by Isabelle Martin in a small workshop in Brooklyn, New York. What began as a passion project — creating the perfect candle for her own home — quickly grew into something much bigger.

Frustrated by mass-produced candles that burned unevenly and used synthetic fragrances, Isabelle set out to create something different. She spent months perfecting her craft, sourcing the finest natural ingredients, and developing unique scent combinations that tell a story.

Today, every Lumière candle is still hand-poured in small batches, using the same artisanal techniques and commitment to quality that Isabelle started with. We've grown, but our heart remains the same.

How We Work

Our Process

Every candle goes through a meticulous process from concept to your home

01

Sourcing

We source the finest natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and sustainable materials from trusted suppliers worldwide.

02

Blending

Our master perfumers create unique scent profiles, blending up to 12 different fragrance notes to achieve the perfect aroma.

03

Pouring

Each candle is hand-poured in small batches at the optimal temperature to ensure even burning and maximum scent throw.

04

Curing

Candles cure for a minimum of 2 weeks, allowing the wax and fragrance to fully bind for a superior scent experience.
